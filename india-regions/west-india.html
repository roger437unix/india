West India Tour

A Travel Goldmine

Romantic coastline, dazzling white desert, rich cultural & historical heritage, cosmopolitan cities, lush green ghats and, numerous wildlife sanctuaries paint an appealing picture of the alluring western region of India. Comprising mainly of Gujarat, Maharashtra, Goa and Daman & Diu; each of these state with its varied & diverse culture, traditions & cuisines is an experience in itself! From the Bollywood craze in Maharashtra to Gujarat’s salt pans to the palm fringed beaches of Goa, it’s all to be cherished forever.

Let’s look at some of the captivating destinations of west India

Gujarat
Vibrant Gujarat, the land of Mahatma Gandhi where expansive coastline meets the rambling white desert, is a tourist’s paradise without the usual clich?s. The land of lions & legends; Gujarat with its beautiful temples, pre-historic sites and marvels of contemporary architecture welcomes you with open arms and warm hearts!

Major Attractions

  • Ahmadabad- Sabarmati Ashram- Mahatma Gandhi’s abode, ISKCON Temple, Swaminarayan Temple, Bhadra Fort, Calico Museum of Textile, Auto World Vintage Car Museum
  • Junagarh- Girnar Mountain, Uparkot Fort, Sakkarbaugh Zoo, Jama Masjid.
  • Rani Ki Vav, Patan- An 11th century beautiful step-well in Patan, now preserved as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Somnath Temple- Exquisitely carved shore temple in the sleepy hamlet of Somnath.
  • Dwarkadhish Temple- Dedicated to Lord Krishna, the temple also alludes to the rich underwater city of Dwarka mentioned in Mahabharata.
  • Dholavira- ruins of Harappan civilization.
  • Lothal- Ruins of Indus Valley civilization.
  • Beaches- The unexplored beaches of Tithal, Gopnath and Hajira.
  • Gir National Park- Home to Asiatic lions, Marine National Park, Jamnagar.
  • Rann of Kutch- The largest salt desert in the world.
  • Tie & die Bandhani handloom, exquisite beadwork, Patola textiles, Zari embroidery, block printing, camel leather artifacts and mud glasswork of Kutch.
  • Festivals- Navratri, Uttarayan (kite festival), Modhera Dance Festival and Rann Festival.?

Daman & Diu
Once governed by the Portuguese, Daman & Diu is still infused with Portuguese history & architecture. Resilient forts, pretty beaches and crumbling Portuguese villas & churches set the tone for Daman & Diu. A beautiful land with lots of sun & sea, Daman & Diu make a perfect beach retreat.

Major Attractions in Daman

  • Moti Daman Fort & Lighthouse
  • Se Cathedral Daman
  • Church of Bom Jesus
  • Jampore Beach
  • Devika Beach
  • Mirasol Lake Garden
  • Parasailing & water sports on beach.

  • Diu Fort & Lighthouse
  • Jalandhar Beach
  • Diu Museum
  • Naida Caves
  • St. Paul’s Church

Maharashtra
Maharashtra is a sprawling canvass depicting glorious tales of a vibrant culture, fascinating attractions, rich history and delightful destinations. It’s lovely palm fringed beaches, lush hills, stunning vineyards, impressive National Parks, UNESCO-listed attractions, a glamorous film industry and bustling cosmopolitan cities promise you a perfect travel experience.

Major Attractions

  • Mumbai- Gateway of India, Elephanta Island & caves, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya, Juhu Beach, Marine Drive.
  • Aurangabad- Ajanta & Ellora Rock Caves, Kailash Temple, Bibi Ka Maqbara, Daulatabad Fort
  • Beaches- Alibaug, Tarkali, Kashid, Ganapatipule, Dahanu- Bordi beach, Mandwa & Kihim Beach.
  • Hill Stations- Panchgani, Lonavla, Khandala, Mahabaleshwar, Matheran
  • Pilgrimage- Shirdi, Shani Shingnapur Temple, Haji Ali Dargah, Siddhivinayak Temple
  • Wildlife- Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Tadoba Andhari National Park, Bhimashankar Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Traditional Paithani Sarees, exquisite Warli paintings, colorful Kolhapuri Sandals and intricate metal work of Bidriware.
  • Festivals & Fairs- Gudhi Padwa (Lunar New Year), Ganesha Chaturthi, Ellora Festival, Kala Ghoda Art Festival. ?

Goa
Goa, the beloved sunshine state of India is a favorite among travelers across the world. Miles of sand, pretty beach shacks, quirky flea markets, elegant churches, melodious strands of soulful music and delicious fish curry define the essence of Goa.

Major Attractions

  • Beaches- Calangute, Baga, Vagator, Palolem, Anjuna, Arambol, Colva
  • Fort Aguada
  • Fontainhas ? Latin quarters of Goa
  • Dudhsagar Falls
  • Basilica of Bom Jesus
  • Flea markets- Anjuna Beach and Saturday Night Bazaar near Baga Beach.
  • Riverfront Casinos
  • Adventure sports like parasailing, banana boat rides, bumpy rides, surfing, paragliding, and speed boating.
  • Goan port wine and Feni- the local spirit of Goa.
  • Goa Carnival
