East India Tour

A Spectacular Bonanza

The eastern region of India is a kaleidoscope of colors and cultures. Ever-changing landscapes, cultural riches and historical & religious structures dominate the east of India. Bustling metropolis & tribal villages, concrete jungles & mangrove forests, gushing waterfalls & serene beaches, temples & stupas and a variety of delectable cuisines; east India is a treasure trove of destinations & attractions.

Let's look at some of the fascinating destinations of the east

Bihar- Buddhism’s Landmark
Bihar, the realm of Buddha, takes its name from the word “Vihara” meaning abode and symbolizing a monastery. An ancient seat of learning, Bihar is home to various monasteries, monuments and historical & religious sites. Mahabodhi Temple and ruins of Nalanda are the famous UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Bihar.

Major Attractions of Bihar

  • Bodh Gaya- Maha Bodhi Temple marks the spot where Buddha gained enlightenment under a Bodhi tree.
  • Nalanda- Archeological ruins of a major seat of learning from 5th to 12th century. It was plundered & destroyed by the Muslim invaders.??
  • Vaishali- Birthplace of Lord Mahavira, the last Jain preacher. Ashokan Pillar marks the site of Buddha’s last sermon.
  • Rajgir- Vishwa Shanti Stupa, Vultures Peak, Cyclopean Wall.
  • Sasaram- Emperor Sher Shah Suri’s Mausoleum.
  • Harminder Sahib- Holy place for the Sikhs.
  • Weaves of Bhagalpur, Madhubani paintings, zardozi embroidery, Mithila paintings, terracotta artifacts & jewelry.
  • Fairs & Festivals- Shravani Mela, Sonepur Cattle Fair, Rajgir Mahotsav- festival of dance & music.
  • Cuisine- Litti Chokha, Khaja, Maal Pua.

Odisha- Serene & Spiritual
This land of lush paddy fields, silvery rivers & lakes, golden sands, tumbling waterfalls, glorious temples and tribal people is a major tourist attract in East India.

Major Attractions of Odisha

  • Bhubaneswar- Parasurameswara Temple, Mukteswar Temple, Lingaraja Temple, Udayagiri & Khandgiri Caves, Nandankanan Zoological Park, Odisha State Museum.
  • Puri- Jagannath Temple, Puri Beach, Chilika Lake & Wildlife Sanctuary, Gundicha Ghar Temple, Atharnala Bridge, sand sculptures on beach.
  • Konark- Sun Temple, Konark Beach, Konark Museum, Ramachandi Temple, Balukhand-Konark Wildlife Sanctuary, beach sand sculptures.
  • Tussar Silk & Sambalpuri silk fabric, wooden artifacts, Dhokra Metal ware & jewelry, stone sculpture, Patachitra or palm leaf paintings.
  • Fairs & Festivals- Jagannath Chariot Festival, Kalinga Mahotsav, Chandan Yatra, Konark Dance Festival and Puri Beach Festival.

Jharkhand- Bounties of Nature
For those looking forward to a tryst with nature, Jharkhand is a perfect travel destination. Copious natural beauty, gorgeous temples and various ethnic tribes lend to the beauty of Jharkhand

Major Attractions of Jharkhand

  • Ranchi- Capital of Jharkhand. Hundru Falls, Dassam Falls, Jonah Falls, Paanch Gagh Falls, Ranchi Lake, Birsa Zoological Park, Kanke Dam, Sun Temple, and Rock Garden.
  • Jamshedpur- Founded by Jamsedji Nusserwanji Tata. Dalma Hills, Jubilee Park & Lake, Jayanti Sarovar, Tata Steel Zoological Park, Tribal Culture Centre, Bhuvneshwari Temple.
  • Hazaribagh- Canary Hill, Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary, Isko Village, Suryakund, Rajarappa Temple, Urwan Tourist Complex, Konar Dam.
  • Deogarh- Basukinath, Trikuta Hills, Shivganga, Rikhia Yogashram, Nandan Pahar, Kundeshwari, Ajgaibinath.
  • Giridih- Parashnath Hills, Usri Falls, Khandoli Park and Dam, Madhuvan, Jharkhandi Dham, Surya Temple, Devari Temple.
  • Bokaro-Steel City of India. Garga Dam, Jawaharlal Nehru Biological Park, Gayatri Mandir, Noori Masjid, Aaiyappa Mandir.
  • Adventure activities like paragliding, parasailing, rock climbing and kayaking at Giridih.
  • Paitkar or scroll paintings, wooden artifacts, bamboo baskets & furniture, metal artifacts and stone carved objects.
  • Fairs & Festivals- Barura Sharif Fair, Belgada Mela, Kundri Mela, Sarhul, Jawa, Hal Punhya, Chhath Puja.

West Bengal- A Cultural Extravaganza
India’s cultural crucible; West Bengal is a mish mash of busy cities, ancient ruins, gorgeous countryside and heritage buildings. From its lively capital Kolkata to the magnificent Kanchenjunga views from Kalimpong, and from the mangrove delta at the Bay of Bengal to the temples of Bishnupur; West Bengal is a mish-mash of all things beautiful!

Major Attractions of West Bengal

  • Kolkata- Howrah Bridge, Victoria Memorial, Kali Ghat Temple, Indian Museum, Birla Temple, Shanti Niketan
  • Darjeeling- Singalila National Park, Tiger Hill, Observatory Hill, Darjeeling Toy Train, Japanese Peace Pagoda
  • Kalimpong- Deolo Hill, Kalimpong Cactus Nursery, Zong Dog Palri Fo Brang Monastery
  • Bishnupur- Rasmanch, Jor-Bangla Temple, Madan-Mohan Temple, Radhe-Govind Temple
  • Gaur Pandua Ruins- Ruins of Hindu, Buddhist and Muslim eras
  • Mandarmani Beach- Beautiful beach in East Midnapore district
  • Sundarban National Park- Home to the Royal Bengal Tiger, mangrove forests and rare crocodiles
  • River rafting, trekking, hiking in Darjeeling and Kalimpong
  • Fairs & Festivals- Durga Puja, Rash Mela, Poush Mela, Teesta Tea & Tourism Festival, Gangasagar Mela

This walkthrough was just a quick preview into some of the many fascinating destinations & attractions of Eastern India. A delightful rendezvous with its enchanting landscapes, rich heritage, remarkable architecture, dense forests and friendly people is all you need for a perfect travel experience.
