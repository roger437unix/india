Kashmir Tour

The Crowning Glory of India

The land where heavens meet the earth, where snowcapped mountains cradle lush valleys, where markets float on the sparkling waters of Dal, where Gods live high up in the mountains and surreal views elate you at every corner- Jammu & Kashmir beckon you to this glorious Paradise on Earth.

From incredible natural beauty and famous pilgrimages to enchanting rides on the Shikaras and adrenalin racing adventure activities, this gorgeous land is a mixed bag of splendid surprises.

Jammu- Abode of Gods
Mountains and adventure, temples and palaces, handicrafts and dry fruits- get ready for a rollercoaster holiday in the winter capital of Jammu & Kashmir.

Major Attractions

  • Vaishno Devi Temple- Holy Hindu shrine
  • Raghunath Temple- Temple dedicated to Lord Rama
  • Peer Kho Cave- Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva
  • Bheemgarh Fort
  • Baag-e-Bahu- Beautiful gardens hosting the largest underground aquarium in the country
  • Panch Peer Dargah
  • Patni Top- Skiing, trekking and picturesque vistas
Get Free Tour QuotesView Tour Packages


Srinagar- Himalayan Heaven
Lofty mountains, yawning valleys, shimmering lakes dotted with traditional houseboats, divine places of worship, ancient ruins and gorgeous architecture- Srinagar recreates the paradise on earth.

Major Attractions

  • Dal Lake- Stay in vintage houseboats, ride the colorful Shikaras and check out the floating market on the lake.
  • Wular Lake- Shikara rides
  • Nagin Lake- Boating, swimming and water skiing
  • Shankaracharya Temple- The ancient seat of Lord Shiva
  • Hazratbal Mosque
  • Mughal Gardens- UNESCO-listed Persian style gardens
  • Awantipora- Ancient ruins of Awantipora kingdom

Gulmarg- A Carpet of Flowers
Kaleidoscopic in spring, lush green in summers, warm orange & brown in autumn and serene white in winter- Gulmarg’s ever-changing colors are an allure for all. Glide down its snow dusted slopes for a wonderful skiing adventure!

Major Attractions

  • Gulmarg Gondola- Cable Car ride
  • Gulmarg Golf Course- Highest golf course in the world?
  • Baba Reshi’s Shrine
  • St. Mary’s Church- Trekking trail to a beautiful church
  • Gulmohar Biosphere Reserve
  • Skiing- Skiing courses also offered

Sonmarg- Glittering Gold
The beautiful, flower-filled meadows of Sonmarg invite you on a delightful sojourn. Gushing River Indus, sky-high mountain passes and glistening greens set the tone for Sonmarg.

Major Attractions

  • Lakes- Gadsar, Gangabal, Vishnar, Krishnasar and Satsar
  • Nilgrad River- Its red waters are considered holy and curative?
  • Baltal- Camping grounds for the famous Amarnath Yatra
  • Zozila Pass- High-altitude pass linking Kashmir and Ladakh
  • White water rafting- Battling the waters of River Indus?

Pahalgam- Pristine Pastures
This secluded shepherds’ village with its expansive green meadows, tumultuous River Lidder and gorgeous Sheshnag Lake beckons you for a wonderful Himalayan adventure.

Major Attractions

  • Betaab Valley- Picturesque flower-filled valley
  • Sheshnag Lake
  • Amarnath Cave Temple
  • Aru Valley- Leisurely pony rides, action packed zorbing, zip-lining & power paragliding.
  • White-water rafting in River Lidder

Leh-Ladakh- The Glacial Desert
Leh-Ladakh’s enchanting sights capture you as you descend down serpentine roads to gaping valleys, camp at high-altitude frozen lakes and trek through glaciers & precarious terrains. Oasis of emerald villages, timeless monasteries and speechless silence take over your senses in this beautiful destination.

Major Attractions

  • Monasteries- Hemis Gompa, Spituk Gompa, Diskit Gompa, Alchi Gompa, Phyang Gompa.
  • Lakes- Pangong Tso Lake, Tso Moriri, Tso Kar, Nyak Tso, Yarab Tso, Mirpal Tso
  • Zanskar Valley- River-rafting in Zanskar River in summers and Frozen River Trek in winters.
  • Khardungla Pass- The highest motorable road in the world. ?
  • Nubra Valley- Of mountains and monasteries
  • Magnetic Hill- Beckons you up!
  • Hemis National Park- Call of the Wild.

Fairs & Festivals of J&K

  • Eid-Ul-Fitr and Eid-Ul-Azha
  • Hemis Festival, Leh-ladakh
  • Tulip Festival- Srinagar
  • Shikara Festival- Dal Lake, Srinagar
  • Lohri- Harvest Festival
  • Baisakhi- Spring Festival

Cuisine of Jammu & Kashmir
Food in J&K is a reflection of its many cultures. While Jammu is more about Rajma Chawal, Khatta Gosht, Shiri Pulav and Madra, Kashmir is a paradise for non-vegetarians with its mouthwatering Wazwan- a platter of non-veg dishes, Kashmiri Pulav, Dum Aloo and hot tea- “Kahwa”. Leh-Ladakh serves the taste of Tibet in the form of Thupka, Momos and Tingmo.

Shopping in Jammu & Kashmir
Pashmina shawls and stoles, wood-carved artifacts, Papier Mache stuff, dry fruits, saffron, and Tibetan memorabilia are some of the collectibles from the beautiful state of Jammu & Kashmir.

Best time to visit Jammu & Kashmir
This land is a place for all seasons and all reasons. While the peak season runs from March to October, those looking to ski and enjoy the snow should visit from December to February.

How to Reach
By Air - Srinagar Airport caters to domestic traffic from major Indian cities.
By Train - Jammu Tawi Railway Station, situated at a distance of about 275 km from Srinagar, is the nearest railhead serving Jammu & Kashmir valley.
By Road ? Jammu & Kashmir is well connected to major Indian cities by a network of luxury & deluxe buses.
