Honeymoon Tours in India

Celebrating New Beginnings

Honeymoon tours are celebrations of a new beginning! A holiday to explore, discover and rejoice in the marital bliss; Honeymoon is the golden period for couples to celebrate their bond of love. And the magical land of India; with its exotic destinations, royal culture and breathtaking natural beauty; is a much sought after honeymoon destination for the global travelers. The plunging valleys of Kashmir, the dreamy backwaters of Kerala, the misty mountains of Ooty, the sun-kissed beaches of Goa, the royal heritage of Rajasthan and the azure waters of the Andaman- you are sure to be spoilt for choice!

Kashmir- Epitomizing Romance
One of the most romantic destinations in the world where you can stay in the traditional houseboats on the Dal, enjoy panoramic views, ride the dreamy Shikara, ski down the snow-dusted slopes and rejoice in the blooming meadows.

Udaipur, Rajasthan- A Royal Affair
Indulge like royalty in heritage hotels & havelis, lunch on a Shikara ride on the lake, ride a boat to the charming Lake Palace, watch a glorious sunset and explore the wilderness of Jaisamand with your significant other- the options are myriad and very romantic!

Manali- A Romantic Sojourn
A place lost in time, Manali is a favorite with the honeymooners. Its dense pine forests, narrow mountain trails, delightful sunrise, misty mountains and cascading waterfalls are an allure for romantic couples. The adventure-seekers may head to the nearby Solang Valley for a dose of paragliding, zorbing, skiing and horse riding.

Goa- Love is in the air!
Beautiful beaches and the chilled-out vibe is the insignia of Goa! Explore its quaint colonial buildings, soak in the sun, sip some bubbly at a candle-lit beach shack, bask in the glorious sunsets, go for some water sports or just relax in your luxurious resort- Goa pampers you all the way!

Darjeeling ? A Fling with the Himalayas
A perfect place to breathe in the beauty of the Himalayas, Darjeeling holds a magnetic allure for romantic couples. Glorious sunrise on the Tiger Hill, meandering tea slopes, ancient Mahakal temple, quaint Toy Train, timeless monasteries and expanses of forests beckon to you for a beautiful sojourn in Darjeeling.

Backwaters of Kumarakom/ Alleppey, Kerala- A Romantic Escape
Escape the noise of the city and head to the backwaters for an epic honeymoon. Sail through the backwaters in your ethnic houseboat equipped with modern amenities and plush d?cor and cherish your moments of seclusion and togetherness. Dreamy candle light dinners, rejuvenating massages, delectable fresh sea food and scenic vistas of paddy fields enthrall you on your quiet & scenic honeymoon tour of the backwaters.

Andaman Islands- Courting the Blue
The magnetic allure of azure waters, quiet sandy beaches and striking corals make Andaman an exotic honeymoon destination. Enjoy the scenic beauty, frolic in its warm waters, explore its many islands and be sure to dive underwater to experience the vividly tinted marine life.

Ooty- A snug retreat
Gently rolling hills, dense forests, tumbling waterfalls and verdant tea slopes; all echo of love and romance. Stay in colonial plantation estates, walk the romantic tea trails and bond over a cup of steaming brew as you discover more about each other.

The white sands of Jaisalmer, the cold desert of Leh & Ladakh, the coffee plantations of Munnar & Coorg, the colonial charm of Dalhousie, Nainital and Shimla, the blue city of Jodhpur, the sensual heritage of Khajuraho and the ethereal beauty of the north eastern states; the destinations are countless!
