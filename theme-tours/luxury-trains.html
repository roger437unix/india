Major Luxury Trains in India

Palace on Wheels- A Royal Romance on Rails!
The first and one of the world’s most renowned trains, the Palace on Wheels, is a royal saga narrated in regal splendor. As you step onboard, you feel like royalty and are indulged like one! The Palace on Wheels takes you on a magical journey across the royal palaces, forts and exotic wildlife of Rajasthan. Its sojourn in Agra, the land of the glorious Taj Mahal, makes the travel all the more special.


Maharaja Express- A Regal Affair
Another crown jewel in the luxury train circuit, Maharaja Express transports you back to the romance of elegant traveling of the erstwhile Indian Maharajas. Flagged off in 2010, Maharaja Express offers well curated itineraries with a classy blend of culture, heritage and delightful experiences. From the colorful land of Rajasthan to the tranquil backwaters and from the majestic Tal Mahal to ruins of Hampi; each journey is a signature experience!

Buddhist Circuit Train- A Journey towards Enlightenment
This train, also known as the Maha-Pariniravan Express, takes you on a journey to enlightenment in the land of Gautam Buddha. Keeping in lines with the austerity preached by Buddhism, the train is not decked in opulence, but it does offer lavish comfort to the travelers on board. It’s well curated itinerary retraces the steps of Buddha through his birthplace in Lumbini to the site of his enlightenment in Bodh Gaya and from the ruins of Nalanda to the Jetavana Monastery in Sravasti. This beautiful journey ends with a visit to Agra- the Land of Taj Mahal.

Luxury trains offer you not only an opportunity to travel in sheer opulence but also a hassle-free Indian experience. All your needs including accommodation, dining, guided excursions & organized shopping trips are tended to; and you are free to bask in the glorious & colorful heritage of India.
