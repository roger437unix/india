India Golden Triangle Tour

Experience the Indian Glory

A perfect introduction to incredible India; from the magnificent colonial & Mughal architecture in Delhi to the epitome of love in Agra and the royal heritage of Jaipur; the Golden Triangle tour takes you on a time travel through the glorious history of India.

This tour includes three of the most emblematic cities of India ? Delhi, Agra and Jaipur; which when plotted on the map form a rough equilateral triangle; which is termed golden due to the splendid attractions in the tour.

Delhi
Delhi, the modern capital of India, is a perfect fusion of ancient & modern. Conquered, plundered & rebuilt by numerous invaders, this timeless city is a blend of seven older cities & cultures. The colorful chaos of Old Delhi, the structured edifices of Lutyen’s Delhi and the crumbling heritage of the bygone empires, all stand together, narrating the legends of its many cities. The Mughal splendor of the Red Fort, the architectural genius of Humayun’s Tomb, the tranquility of Rajghat, the hustle-bustle of Chandni Chowk and its many delectable street foods give you a quick taste of this eclectic city.

Jaipur
Next on the Golden Triangle is the pink city of Jaipur; the gateway to the colorful land of the royals. A city resplendent with magnificent palaces, resilient forts and chaotic bazaars; Jaipur transports you into the regal era of Rajasthan. Walk along the strong bastions of Amber Fort, delight in the exquisite design of the Hawa Mahal, marvel at the keen astronomical acumen of Jantar Mantar and check out the royal residence of Jaipur. Spicy street food, colorful fabric, jewelry and leather artifacts are the insignia of Jaipur. Experience the opulent culture of the royals with a heritage property stay in Jaipur.

Agra
The most awaited of all; Agra, rising on the banks of the river Yamuna, is synomous with Taj Mahal- one of the Seven Wonders of the World! Built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan who poured all his love, treasures and passion in creating this architectural marvel in the memory of his beloved wife; Taj Mahal has stood over the centuries as an immortal symbol of love and loss. Taj Mahal certainly is the centerpiece but Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri are fantastic illustrations of Mughal architecture in India. A perfect place to experience the culture & cuisine of the Mughals, Agra is an unparallel vortex on the Golden Triangle Tour.

A complete circuit of the Golden Triangle of India is a riveting experience of Indian history and culture. Though India is a huge land and its diversities are equally complex & fascinating, Golden Triangle Tour gives you an opportunity to delve into the bulk of it to understand the essence of India.
