Ayurveda & Yoga Destinations of India

Kerala
Kerala is synonymous with Ayurveda. Its moist humid climate, plentiful supply of medicinal plants & herbs and generations of certified practitioners of Ayurveda, makes it the Ayurveda capital of the world. Its scenic locales are home to luxury Ayurveda resorts where you can undergo short term rejuvenating therapies or elaborate Ayurvedic remedies. Each therapy is based on the individual body type and requirement.

Let's check out some of the wellness and rejuvenation therapies practiced in Kerala:

  • Body immunization and longevity treatment
  • Rejuvenation Therapy or Body Purification Therapy
  • Slimming Program
  • Stress Management Program
  • Beauty Care Program
  • Ayurveda Anti-Aging Program

Uttarakhand
On the foothills of the Himalayas, the two sacred towns of Haridwar and Rishikesh welcome you to the healing touch of Ayurveda. Amidst majestic mountains and tranquil Ganges, there are various luxurious Ayurveda and Yoga resorts that offer many wellness programs. You can choose a short term rejuvenation program or a detailed therapy, depending upon the ailment.


Rishikesh, with its ancient heritage, is also known as the Yoga capital of India. The many ashrams and yoga retreats here practice and teach the art of Yoga. Postures, breathing, relaxation, meditation, and dietary habits form the basis of Yoga courses. Short drop-in classes as well extensive yoga courses are available here.

Other Ayurveda & Yoga Destinations in India
Besides, these two major destinations, there are various Ayurveda & Yoga retreats across the country. Pune, Udaipur, Pondicherry, Chennai, Mahabalipuram and Goa are some other destinations where luxury hotels and resorts offer yoga, meditation, Ayurveda and alternate spa therapies to the travelers. Some of the alternate spa therapies include aromatherapy massages, body wraps, mud baths and steam baths.
