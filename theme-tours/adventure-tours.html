Adventure Tours in India

Get the Adrenalin Going!!

An adventure buff would certainly be spoilt for choices in India! The diverse topography comprising of lofty mountains, fast flowing rivers, glaciers, beautiful valleys, rich forest reserves, teeming corals and glittering sands of Thar offer umpteen options to thrill-seekers for a perfect adventure holiday.

Let's look at some of the bucket list ideas for adventure travelers in India:

White Water Rafting- Combating the Frothy Waters
Battling the fury of chilling waters of a gushing mountain river is a daredevil’s delight. With various grades of rapids, from easy ones in the Ganges in Rishikesh to the challenging rapids of Zanskar in Ladakh; river rafting in India is open for experts as well as rookies for some adrenalin pumping fun.

Ladakh, Rishikesh, Kashmir, Manali, Kalimpong, Orchha and Dandeli are some of the major river-rafting destinations in India.

Trekking & Camping- Walk the Picturesque Land
From trekking across a frozen river to climbing up lush forest trails and from exploring the barren cold desert to retracing the routes of mountain rivers; the trekking & camping options in India are simply endless.

Valley of Flowers Trek in Uttarakhand, Roopkund and Stok Kangri Treks in Himachal, Frozen River Trek and Snow Leopard Trail in Ladakh and Yuksom-Goech la Trek in Sikkim are some of the well-known trekking routes in India.

Skiing- Glide Down the Alpine Meadows
The snow dusted Himalayan slopes in India are a paradise for ski-lovers. An assortment of gradients from easy slopes in Naldhera and Auli to the precarious ones in Gulmarg, offer a range of experiences for all kinds of skiing aficionados.

Gulmarg in Kashmir, Manali, Naldhera and Kufri in Himachal and Auli in Uttarakhand are some of the awe-inspiring skiing destinations in India.

Jungle Safari- Spot the Big Game
The thrill of waiting and catching a glimpse of a lion, tiger or a rhino running in abandon in a dense jungle is something you must try at least once in life. Thousands of thrill-seekers from all across the world come to India for a hair-raising experience in the Indian wilderness.

Tiger safaris in Jim Corbett National Park, Bandhavgarh and Ranthambore; Lion Safari in Gir; Kaziranga Rhino Safari and Periyar Elephant Safari are some of the thrilling jungle adventures in India.

Mountaineering- Scale the Himalayan Heights
The snow bound Himalayan peaks in India are a major lure for the mountaineers across the world. Various mountaineering institutes like Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and Nehru Institute of Mountaineering offer basic and advance level training to enthusiasts willing to scale the Himalayan heights.

Nanda Devi, Trishul and Kamet Peaks in Uttarakhand, Mt. Jopunu, Mt. Thingchinkhang and Mt. Pandim in Sikkim, Kharcha Parabat, White Sail, Menthos Peaks in Himachal and Kangyatse Peak in Ladakh are favorites amongst the mountaineers.

Desert Safari- Exploring Desert Kingdom
One of the most exhilarating and memorable experiences you can have in India is to ride through the shifting sands of Thar desert on camel back and camp out under a star spangled sky. Guided mostly by stars and reading the patterns of perpetually veering desert sands, the caravan leaders steer you through the desert just like the desert nomads.

Jaisalmer, Jodhpur, Bikaner in Rajasthan are part of the desert circuit in India.

Water Sports- Discover the Mysteries of the Ocean
Flanked by the Bay of Bengal in the east and the Arabian Sea in the west, the extensive coastline of India is a delight for water sports junkies. From surfing & jet skiing to speed boating & lagoon canoeing to kayaking & water scooter riding, snorkeling & scuba diving- the options are very exciting.

Goa, Andaman & Nicobar Islands & Lakshadweep Islands are just perfect for some thrilling action-packed water sports holiday.
